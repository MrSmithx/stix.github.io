Holistic Counselling

Holistic Counselling is a little different from other more mainstream counselling styles.
Holistic Counselling treats a person holistically as a WHOLE PERSON referring to the MIND. BODY and SPIRIT. The Holistic Counsellor's goal is to asist you to bring balance into your whole life and to ensure that you are in touch with your own needs on a holistic level.

Holistic Life Coaching

What is a Holistic Life Coach?

A Holistic Life Coach is someone who is committed to helping people in every aspect of their life by transforming their mind, body and spirit. Holistic Life Coaching is more focused on connecting these three aspects of your life so you can live even more intentionally and more mindfully. It covers core areas in relation to the whole so you can truly heal, be complete and move forward.

Holistic Counselling:

As your Holistic Counsellor I will hold space for you offering unconditional support. Opening my heart and letting go of all judgement and control. I will support you wherever you are on your journey without trying to fix you, judge you, control the outcome or make you feel less then you truly are… which is whole.

We will explore mindful appreciation of what contributes to emotional, spiritual and mental health via science-based facts, self-enquiry, application of mind body practices, visualisation exercises, multiple breathing techniques, meditation and the power of thoughts affecting the physical health of the body.

I will listen to you, holding space, allowing you to ‘unload’ without trying to rush or correct you.

I will ask powerful questions to help you gain a thorough understanding of...
- where you have been in the past
- where you are now
- and where you want to go moving forward in your life.

I will help you to recognize patterns that have possibly been the cause of difficulties for you in the past.

Assist you to choose new more positive directions for yourself.

I will help you develop lifelong skills that empower you to take control of your own lives, permanently.

I am here as your holistic counsellor to help you choose the changes you wish to make in your life.

While Holistic Counselling recognizes the value of past experiences that have contributed to the development of a person’s current situation, it is about letting go of the past, moving forward and not rehashing old traumas.

I am here as your Holistic Counsellor to help you choose the changes you wish to make in your lives. I will only need to help you overcome old erroneous subconscious belief systems, when and if those beliefs are blocking you from moving forward into the happier, healthier life you want to achieve for yourselves.

As a Holistic Life Coach, I will be working within the Holistic Framework using practical neuroscience, positive psychology, and mind-body practices all scientifically evidence-based strategies to your advantage.

Holistic Life Coaching helps you to see your life as a whole and not just as individual parts. This type of Life Coaching helps you to activate your power, access your true potential and helps you to make transformational changes in your life so that eventually, you start to create a life that you desire for yourself.

Anyone who is feeling unfulfilled in any area of their life can gain from Holistic Life Coaching.

As your Holistic Life Coach, I will support you:
- to improve your relationships, resolve conflicts and set healthy boundaries.
- to empower, encourage and motivate you.
- to find your true potential.
- in awakening you to the limitless possibilities of your life that you never knew existed.
- to overcome barriers and limiting beliefs.
- to manage stress and lead a healthy and balanced life.
- to guide you in setting achievable goals.
- and motivate you to work toward reaching your goals.
- and monitor your progress and help you to set and achieve more goals or to reassess and reset goals as they advance.
- To remove the blocks that are stopping you from realising your true power that helps you to become clear about what you want. Once you’ve gained clarity, creating a life that you love becomes so much easier.
