Services

Holistic Wellbeing Central Coast offers a range of services to help you feel unstuck in this unpredictable world, allowing you to connect with your true power and to assist with navigating the challenges of life.

My aim is to instil valuable life skills and tools to carry with you into the future.

Available Services

Holistic Counselling

A unique form of counselling, focusing on a person in their entirety. It considers physiological and psychological disorders as part of a greater whole, and regards the mind, and spirit as fundamentally interconnected.

Life Coaching

Life Coaching looks to empower the individual by helping them make, meet and exceed goals in both their personal and professional lives.

Teaching Meditation

Meditation allow an individual to use techniques to focus the mind on a particular object, thought, or activity, to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state.

Stress Management

Providing a wide range of techniques and psychotherapies aimed at controlling an individual's level of stress, especially chronic stress, to improve everyday functioning.
