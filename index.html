Holistic Counselling considers a fourth element to the wellbeing equation and that is Emotion.

Flight / Fight Response

Most of us live in long periods of being in Flight / Fight response without even knowing it. This has detrimental impacts on our emotional, mental, physical and spiritual wellbeing.

When we are in a state of homeostasis we function more clearly, there is increased benefit to our overall wellbeing and on our life.

Holistic Counselling

Holistic Counselling is a little different from other more mainstream counselling styles.
Holistic Counselling treats a person holistically as a WHOLE PERSON referring to the MIND. BODY and SPIRIT. The Holistic Counsellor's goal is to assist you to bring balance into your whole life and to ensure that you are in touch with your own needs on a holistic level.

Holistic Life Coaching

What is a Holistic Life Coach?

A Holistic Life Coach is someone who is committed to helping people in every aspect of their life by transforming their mind, body and spirit. Holistic Life Coaching is more focused on connecting these three aspects of your life so you can live even more intentionally and more mindfully. It covers core areas in relation to the whole so you can truly heal, be complete and move forward.

Holistic Counselling:

As your Holistic Counsellor I will hold space for you offering unconditional support. Opening my heart and letting go of all judgement and control. I will ask powerful questions to help you gain a thorough understanding of...
