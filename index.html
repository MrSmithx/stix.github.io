Holistic healing is based on the theory that one element of a person cannot be healthy without all of the other aspects of the person being in balance and harmony. For example, the mind will not be well if the body is not well; the body will not be well if the spirit is not well.

The three aspects of a person we mainly hear about in holistic healing are mind, body and spirit (or soul). Each of these three aspects could be built on or dissected, to give us an even more detailed and accurate picture of the many different parts that make up a person on a holistic level. How we view or categorise these different aspects is going to depend on our own personal, spiritual or religious belief systems. Because our modality of healing is not based on religious foundations (while we always remain respectful and accepting of the religious beliefs of others and may certainly hold strong personal beliefs ourselves), our roles as counsellors is to guide our clients toward their own ultimate goals, not provide religious education or spiritual enlightenment; though the attainment of both is often the result of achieving holistic balance for any person as a natural course.

For the purpose of clarity as a Holistic Counsellor we will consider a fourth element to the holistic wellbeing equation and that is emotion. So, we have mind, body, spirit and emotion. When all of these aspects of a person are in balance, we find a happy, healthy individual. When one, or more, of these aspects are out of balance, we find ill health, unhappiness and more often a life filled with an ongoing string of dissatisfaction, failure and one personal disaster closely followed by another.

While Holistic Counselling recognizes the value of past experiences that have contributed to the development of a person’s current situation, it is about letting go of the past, moving forward and not rehashing old traumas.

I am here as your holistic counsellor to help you choose the changes you wish to make in your lives. I will only need to help you overcome old erroneous subconscious belief systems, when and if those beliefs are blocking you from moving forward into the happier, healthier life you want to achieve for yourselves.
