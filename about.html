Holistic Wellbeing Therapist

Leanne Smith

Here to support you as your Holistic Wellbeing Therapist. Supporting you to feel unstuck, connect with your true power again navigating life and its challenges head on feeling confident. Instilling valuable lifelong tools to carry with you into the future.

I have a background working in Adult Social Work and the Social Care/ Community Welfare field for the past 15 years.

I have successfully attained The International Meditation Teachers Therapists Association (IMTTA) Certificate in:

• Holistic Counselling and Holistic Life Coach

• Meditation Teacher and Holistic Human Development

• Chair Yoga Instruction

I am also currently completing my Diploma in Meditation Therapy and Holistic Counselling.
